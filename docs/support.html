Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a birthday?

Tap the "+" button on the Home screen. Enter the person's name, birthday, and any optional details like photo, relationship, interests, or notes. Tap Save.

How do notifications work?

Giftly sends three reminders for each birthday: 7 days before, 1 day before, and on the day at 9 AM. These repeat yearly. You can also tap "Call", "Message", or "Gift Ideas" directly from the notification.

How do I import birthdays from my contacts?

Go to Settings → there's no direct import button in the current version, but you can add people manually. Contact import is available as a privacy-minimized feature that only accesses names, birthdays, and photos — no phone numbers or emails.

How do AI gift suggestions work?

AI suggestions use a "Bring Your Own Key" model. You provide your own API key (any OpenAI-compatible endpoint) in Settings → AI Configuration. The AI Add-on ($5.99 one-time) unlocks the AI feature. Your API key is stored securely in Keychain and never sent to us.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings → Upgrade → "Restore Purchases" button. This will restore any previously purchased Pro or AI Add-on on the same Apple ID.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Export Backup. This creates a JSON file with all your people, gift ideas, and gift history. You can share it via the iOS share sheet. To restore, use Settings → Import Backup.

Is there a subscription?

No. Giftly uses one-time purchases only. No subscriptions, no auto-renewal, no trial periods. Ever.

Managing Your Purchases

If you've purchased Giftly Pro or the AI Add-on, you can manage or restore your purchases:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap Apple ID (your name at the top)
  3. Tap Media & Purchases → View Account
  4. Sign in if prompted
  5. Scroll down to Purchase History to view your purchases

To restore purchases within Giftly: Settings → Upgrade → "Restore Purchases".
