## Design Rationale

The gift box icon directly communicates the app's core function: gift and birthday management. The gradient from lavender to coral matches the brand color system defined in the operation guide. The flat, bold design follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines for app icons — simple, recognizable at small sizes, no text.
